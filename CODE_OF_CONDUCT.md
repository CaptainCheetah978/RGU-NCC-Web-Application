# Code of Conduct

As a project built for the National Cadet Corps (NCC), this repository operates under the same expectations of discipline, respect, and professionalism as the organization itself. 

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Our Expectations (The NCC Standard)
Whether you are a Cadet, an ANO, or an outside developer contributing code, we expect behavior that contributes to a positive, disciplined environment:
1. **Maintain Military Decorum**: Be respectful and constructive when reviewing other people's code or submitting issues.
2. **Demonstrate Empathy**: Treat differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences with kindness.
3. **Accept Responsibility**: Gracefully accept constructive feedback, apologize to those affected by mistakes, and learn from them.
4. **Follow Protocol**: Do not use this platform to dispute organizational policies, and always focus on what is best for the overall unit and open-source community.

### Unacceptable Behavior
Instances of unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated and include:
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or military setting.

### Enforcement Responsibilities and Guidelines
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Violations will be handled using the following enforcement ladder:
1. **Correction**: A private, written warning providing clarity around the nature of the violation.
2. **Warning**: A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior.
3. **Temporary Ban**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication within the repository.
4. **Permanent Ban**: A permanent ban for demonstrating a pattern of violation. **Note:** Serious violations by currently serving NCC cadets may additionally be escalated to their respective unit's ANO for institutional disciplinary action.

**Reporting**: Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported directly to the project maintainers (e.g., Aditya Singh). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, while strictly respecting the privacy and security of the reporter.
